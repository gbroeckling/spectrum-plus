# Bill of Materials (BOM)

This BOM reflects the **known-good** build used for this project.

## Core electronics

- ESP32-S3 DevKitC-1
- HUB75 RGB Matrix panel, 128×64
  - Config uses `shift_driver: FM6126A`
- INMP441 I2S microphone module

## Power

- 5V power supply sized for your panel
- 5V/GND power wiring for the panel (thicker gauge recommended)

## Wiring / mounting

- HUB75 adapter/breakout (optional but recommended)
- Dupont leads / JST cables / screw terminal wiring (whatever matches your hardware)
- Standoffs / enclosure hardware
